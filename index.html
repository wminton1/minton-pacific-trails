Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trail Resort on the California North Coast offers panoramic views of the ocean. Our guest experience is an opulent camping adventure in sumptuously appointed private yurts featuring a gas fireplace, king size bed and private deck.

  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
  • Start the day with a complientary cooked-to-order breakfast
  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary beverages and appetizers
  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view
  • Or just lay around and waste the money you spent on this vaction
